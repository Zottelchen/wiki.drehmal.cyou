# Kiln of Virtuo

--8<-- "under_construction.md"

The **Kiln of Virtuo**, often referred to simply as the **Kiln**, is the most sacred place of the Goddess [[Virtuo]], located on a turquoise isle just to the west of [[Firteid]]. It is home to Virtuo's [[Devotion]] altar, several prominent smaller points of interest, custom merchants, and expansive lore books. The Kiln can be accessed either from the long bridge that connects it to Firteid int he east, or via its southern dock.
