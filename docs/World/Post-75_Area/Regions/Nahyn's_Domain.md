**Nahyn's Domain** is one of 14 Aspect Domains on [[Lo'Dahr]], belonging to the Summit-Drake [[Nahyn]], Aspect of Mountains. This region comprises multiple sub-regions, including three snow-capped peaks, coniferous forest, and a small open meadow. The region sits near the geographic center of Lo'Dahr's habitable area, slightly to the east of the midline, and bordering several other regions.
